# Comprehensive Financial Modeling & Valuation: Hindustan Unilever (FMCG Sector)

## 📌 Executive Summary
Conducted an end-to-end financial analysis and valuation of Hindustan Unilever (HUL). Built a fully integrated 3-statement model forecasting 5-year financial performance, supplemented by Ratio Analysis and Comparable Company Benchmarking. The analysis highlights HUL’s robust profitability, low-risk capital structure, and defensive business model. 

* **Key Finding:** HUL emerges as a cash-rich compounder with net profit scaling from ₹4,476 Cr to ₹15,040 Cr, justifying its premium market valuation through superior cash flows and margin expansion.

## 🛠️ Methodology & Scope
* **3-Statement Modeling:** Reconstructed historical financials and projected P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow statements up to 2031.
* **Comparable Company Analysis (Comps):** Benchmarked valuation multiples (EV/EBITDA, P/E, EV/Revenue) against industry peers including ITC and Hindustan Foods.
* **Trend & Common Size Analysis:** Mapped operating cycle ratios, gross profit margins, and working capital efficiency.
* **Strategic Risk Assessment:** Evaluated leverage, liquidity, and macroeconomic risks (input cost inflation, regulatory shifts) using CIMA strategic frameworks.

## 📊 Key Financial Insights
1. **Profitability & Resilience:** Maintained consistent top-line growth (reaching ₹64,468 Cr) alongside disciplined operational cost management, resulting in strong operating leverage.
2. **Balance Sheet Quality:** Substantial reserve accumulation (₹48,504 Cr) with virtually zero reliance on high-interest debt, ensuring maximum financial flexibility.
3. **Cash Flow Dynamics:** Free Cash Flow generation more than doubled over the historical period, supporting continuous reinvestment and strong dividend payouts.

## 📂 Deliverables Included in this Repository
* `HUL Financial Statement Model.xlsx`: The raw, fully-linked financial model containing all projections, dynamic scenarios, and ratio calculations.
* `HUL Financial Statement Model Report.docx`: A 2-page investment memorandum detailing strategic outlook, competitive advantages, and final valuation thesis.
